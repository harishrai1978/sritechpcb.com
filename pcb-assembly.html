Assembly Types We Offer

Comprehensive assembly solutions for all your needs

SMT Assembly

Surface Mount Technology assembly for compact, high-density PCBs with automated pick-and-place machines.

  • 0201 to large component sizes
  • Automated optical inspection
  • Lead-free and leaded processes

Through-Hole Assembly

Traditional through-hole assembly for robust connections and components requiring mechanical strength.

  • Manual and wave soldering
  • Axial and radial components
  • Connectors and large components

BGA Assembly

Ball Grid Array assembly for high-performance ICs and complex semiconductor packages.

  • X-ray inspection capability
  • Reflow profiling
  • BGA rework and repair
