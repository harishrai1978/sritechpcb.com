About Sritech PCB

Your trusted partner for professional PCB design, assembly, and 3D printing services

Our Story

Founded with a vision to provide comprehensive electronics manufacturing solutions, Sritech PCB Assembly & 3D Printing has grown into a trusted name in the industry. Our journey began with a simple mission: to deliver high-quality PCB services with flexibility and reliability.

Over the years, we've expanded our capabilities to include PCB design, assembly (SMT, Through-hole, BGA), 3D printing, and wiring harness services. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has earned us the ISO 9001:2015 certification, reflecting our dedication to international standards.

Today, we serve a diverse clientele ranging from startups and college students to large multinational corporations, always adapting to their unique needs and timelines.
